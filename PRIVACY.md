# Privacy Policy 

Privacy Policy for Abide Together Last updated: December 2, 2025 Thank you for using Abide Together. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data.

1. Information We Collect - We collect information that you provide directly when using the app, including:
Account Information - Name, Email address, Firebase Authentication User ID (UID) This UID is automatically generated and uniquely identifies your account.

2. Identifiers for Advertising - Device identifiers may be collected by Google AdMob to deliver banner ads.
This includes identifiers such as the Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA) or similar device IDs.

3. User-Generated Content - We store the content you create in the app, including: Calendar events and work days Notes Recipes Prayer selections Saved family or friends that you choose to add. No financial information or sensitive personal information is collected.

4. How We Use Your Information - We use your information to: Create and manage user accounts, Sync app data securely across your devices, Allow optional sharing of data only with family or friends you intentionally add, Improve app functionality and user experience, Provide basic support if you contact us, Display personalized or non-personalized ads using Google AdMob, We do not sell your data to third parties. 

5. Sharing of Information - We do not sell, share, or disclose your personal information to third parties. Information is only visible to others when: You intentionally add family or friends inside the app Those added users gain access to the data you have saved in the app (e.g., shared calendar events, recipes, and notes) At no point is your information visible to the public or to users outside your chosen family/friend connections.

6. Third-Party Services - Abide Together uses trusted third-party services:
     Firebase Authentication - Used for secure account creation and login.
     Firebase Cloud Firestore - Used to store your app data using encryption in transit and at rest. Firebase may           collect certain diagnostic data necessary for secure app functioning. You can read more here:
       https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy.
     Google AdMob - Used for displaying banner advertisements in the app. AdMob may collect device identifiers (such        as IDFA), usage data, and interaction data for personalized advertising. You can learn more about Google’s
       privacy practices here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads.
   
7. Data Security - We take the security of your information seriously. We use: Encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS), Firebase security rules restricting access to authorized users, Device-based authentication through Firebase. Only the developers of Abide Together have access to the backend database for maintenance.

8. Data Retention - Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you wish to delete your data, you may contact us to request full account removal.

9. Your Rights -  You have the right to: Access your personal information, Request correction of inaccurate information, Request deletion of your data, Withdraw from using the app at any time, Limit ad tracking or opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings. You can request any of these by contacting us at the email below.

10. Children’s Privacy - Abide Together is intended for use by families and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent.


11. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated policy will always be posted at this URL.
Contact Us If you have questions, requests, or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact: Abide Together Support: abidetogether.help@gmail.com
